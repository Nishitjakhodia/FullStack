Biography Page

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Early Life

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He came from a humble background and worked hard to become one of India's most respected scientists and leaders.

Achievements

Dr. Kalam was known as the Missile Man of India for his work in ballistic missile and space rocket technology. He later became the 11th President of India and was deeply loved by people across the country.

His book "Wings of Fire" is an inspiration to millions. You can read more about his life on Wikipedia.

My Daily Routine

Morning

I usually wake up around 6:00 AM. After a quick workout and meditation session, I take a shower and eat a healthy breakfast. Then, I start my day by reviewing my to-do list and checking emails.

Afternoon

My afternoon is mostly spent on work or study. I like to break my tasks into small chunks for better focus. Around 1 PM, I take a lunch break, sometimes with friends or family. After lunch, I continue with my projects or assignments.

Evening

In the evening, I relax by reading a book or watching a documentary. I also spend time on hobbies like photography or coding. After dinner, I unwind by journaling and prepare for the next day.
